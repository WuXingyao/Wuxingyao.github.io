Reflection on School Football Team in 2021-2022 Season

Posted on 2022-10-10

In November, 2021, I followed our school football team to play the Area Champion League and I had four opportunities to play for our team. I played on the right half and my task was attacking from wide positions by using my advantages in footwork and speed. In the four games I played, I contributed one assist, numerous dangerous attacks and several shots.

This was my first time to play for our school in formal matches and I thought the reason why I could obtain the opportunity to play was the efforts that I made during the past half year. I did not use to be a member of our football team because I was not a sports specialty student when entering this school. The coach of our school team told us that he could offer me a chance to train with them, but whether or not I could join the team would depend on my performances and progress after training.

I was always the first one to be in the field if there was training that day and maintained full attendance at all training sessions just for improving my footwork and the ability of understanding what to do with the ball in actual combat. My skills in playing football had greatly improved during the half-year training and I ultimately had the competence of playing against those sports specialty students in other schools.

The experience of how I entered our school football team and how I trained in order to obtain the opportunities to play in formal matches literally inspired me since that means I have reached one of my middle-term goals and I believe that will provide me more passion for future progress.



Reflection of leading team to attend National Economics Competition

Posted on 2022-10-10

At the beginning of August, we continuously invited the confirmed Grade 10 students to the QQ group which was established for their convenience to ask us questions about the preparation for the new semester. I noticed that the most common questions they asked were about the preview of subjects and new school life like which textbooks they would use, was it necessary to prepare for the IELTS or TOFEL examinations in the first summer and the studying and living atmosphere and circumstances of our school.

At that time, I was thinking about whether there was a convenient way for them to get to know all the resources in one website which could update continuously for solving their sustaining confusions and providing on-going studying materials and suggestions. There was a famous video platform called Bili Bili, which is a place where you can upload your own videos like YouTube. Therefore, I decided to cooperate with two of my close friends to record videos to help the new integrate into our school more quickly and help them preview the schoolwork of IB with our knowledge.

We started to organize this project on July,14 and shared the link of our videos to every new student for the equal opportunity of the resources. After the setting up and the promotion, we uploaded approximately twenty videos about school life, subjects preview (Economics, Chemistry, Business Management, Biology, IELTS and TOFEL) and special columns like explain real-life phenomena with Economics principles at the end of August. We felt pleased that all the new students, our classmates and our teachers gave us high praises and I thought that we were successful in doing this We Media for the short-term aim that helped the new students.

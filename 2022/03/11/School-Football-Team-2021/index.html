School Football Team 2021

In November, 2021, I followed our school football team to play the Area Champion League and I had four opportunities to play for our team. I played on the right half and my task was attacking from wide positions by using my advantages in footwork and speed. In the four games I played, I contributed one assist, numerous dangerous attacks and several shots.

This was my first time to play for our school in formal matches and I thought the reason why I could obtain the opportunity to play was the efforts that I made during the past half year. I did not use to be a member of our football team because I was not a sports specialty student when entering this school. The coach of our school team told us that he could offer me a chance to train with them, but whether or not I could join the team would depend on my performances and progress after training.

I was always the first one to be in the field if there was training that day and maintained full attendance at all training sessions just for improving my footwork and the ability of understanding what to do with the ball in actual combat. My skills in playing football had greatly improved during the half-year training and I ultimately had the competence of playing against those sports specialty students in other schools.

The experience of how I entered our school football team and how I trained in order to obtain the opportunities to play in formal matches literally inspired me since that means I have reached one of my middle-term goals and I believe that will provide me more passion for future progress.

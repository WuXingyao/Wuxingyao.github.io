Reflection1

Feedback 1:

(1) Forms and styles of presentation should be paid attention to, for example, the speaking speed should not be too fast, and the main point is to make sure listeners are clear.

(2) The content shown is not closely related to the topic. The topic states the research will focus on influences on the industry while the content and the analysis focus on the proof of the existence of influence.

Feedback 2:

(1) Some important and valuable points are not fully explored, for example, business financial reports are not completely analyzed since relative ratios are not calculated.

(2) Focusing too much on tools themselves and the application of these tools instead of clearly explaining why these tools are better for this research.

Feedback 3:

(1) It is valuable and worthy proving the existence of influence, which can be encouraged.

(2) It will be better to find out the similarities and similar trends of stock prices of public limited companies in this industry to draw general conclusions. Specific periods can be sorted out and further explored, for example, which events led to such trend within this period?

(3) The topic is too large to be fully covered, so it can be narrowed.

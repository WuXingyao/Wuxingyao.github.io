(2) 1948: General Agreement on Tariffs and Trade (GATT)

1995: World Trade Organization (WTO)

(3) Labor lobbyists complaining of unfair competition were denounced as protectionist by those who saw cost differentials as legitimate forms of comparative advantage.

(4) Indeed, economic integration was seen as a way to help achieve these other goals. Not only would economies benefit from being bound together, but faster growth would mean higher environmental and labor standards.

(5) Between 1990 and 2017 the trade-weighted average global tariff applied under WTO rules fell by 4.3 percentage points. The drop was greatest in poor countries.

(6) Global trade as a share of gross output, from around 30% in the early 1970s to 60% in the early 2010s. Global supply chains grew from around 37% to 50% of total trade.

(7) The stunning collapse in transport costs boosted international commerce.

(8) Reduction in uncertainty was responsible for around a third of the growth in Chinese exports between 2000 and 2005.

(9) World Bank in 2019: a 1% rise in participation in global value chains is linked with an increase in income per head of more than 1% in the long run.

(10) Douglas Irwin of Dartmouth College: poor countries which liberalized trade enjoyed higher growth of 1-1.5 percentage points, cumulating to 10%-20% after a decade.

(11) The United States International Trade Commission: America’s bilateral and regional trade agreements have raised real incomes by 0.6%.

(12) In November 2020, 15 Asia-Pacific countries signed the Regional Comprehensive Economic Partnership.

(13) In January 1st 2021, African Continental Free Trade Area was ratified by 38 countries.

albeit after erecting large new barriers with its closest neighbor, the European

Union.

(15) America and the EU are discussing common international standards

for the digital economy.

Thoughts

对于“更加自由的贸易是否应该实施”这个问题，不同的人或组织给出了不同的立场，我个人还是更加偏向于freer trade的。原本我想的是，可以研究“本国政府如何制定政策（贸易政策和国内的经济政策）以使得自己的贸易中取得利益最大化”，可后来发现不太现实，因为“贸易”都是在两个，或多个国家之间进行的，但凡其他国家的政府在做决定时也一样理性，每个国家都想使得自己利益最大化最终就会导致大家都worse off。因此，自由贸易，或者说国际贸易的好处就在于能够使得合作双方better off，而不应该想着从合作伙伴身上占便宜。很多经济大国，例如美国，在很多时候都是反对freer trade的，那么特朗普作为一名十分具有商业和经济思维的总统之所以大力反对free trade必然是因为这会给美国带来损失。因此，我们不仅可以针对不同群体（例如被威胁的本国产业）所提出的free trade会带来的问题进行研究，还可以对free trade对不同群体、国家所带来的好处/坏处的差异进行研究。
